Shipping FAQ

When will my order be dispatched?

Orders placed before 3pm Monday-Friday are typically dispatched the same day. Weekend orders are processed on Monday. Bank holidays may cause 1-day delays.

Do you deliver to business addresses?

Yes, we deliver to both residential and business addresses. Business deliveries are typically made between 9am-5pm Monday-Friday.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Our couriers will attempt delivery and leave a card if you're not available. You can arrange redelivery or collection from a local depot through the tracking information provided.

Are there any restricted items?

Some hazardous materials (certain fluids, batteries, airbags) may have shipping restrictions. These will be clearly marked on product pages and alternative shipping arranged where possible.
